Burning Recycling Questions

We've got answers (and no judgment!)

Great question! Unfortunately, pizza boxes with grease/food residue go in the residual waste (black bin). Only clean cardboard can be recycled as paper. Pro tip: Tear off any clean parts for the paper bin!

Plastic bags are Vienna's recycling troublemakers! They don't belong in the yellow bin—take them to collection points at grocery stores (look for the plastic bag recycling bins). Better yet: Use reusable bags!

Aluminum capsules (like Nespresso) can be returned to Nespresso boutique stores. Plastic capsules (like Dolce Gusto) currently go in residual waste. We're lobbying for better solutions—stay tuned!

Give containers a quick rinse—they don't need to be spotless, but major food residue can contaminate recycling. Pro water-saving tip: Use leftover dishwater or rinse while washing other dishes!
